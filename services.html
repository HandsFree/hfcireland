Training, your way from Hands Free Computing

Over the past 22 years, Hands Free Computing have become renowned for delivering first class training and support.

Each person is unique and we believe that offering a variety of training methods according to your learning style gets the best results. Choose your unique blend of training below or speak to one of our consultants to identify what works best for you and the way you work.

Face to Face Sessions Training

We have a long history of providing face to face sessions where we will travel to your location and deliver class leading training at your place of work. These sessions can be delivered in either half days (3 hour) or full days (5 hour) sessions.

Remote Sessions

Given the current situation around the world, we are able to offer remote sessions through a variety of remote conferencing platforms to best ensure we find the best one for your organisation.

Any remote sessions are offered with a complimentary 15-minute set-up call with one of our experienced technicians. This ensures any potential issues are resolved prior to a session to maximise your training time.

Value Added Combination Training Packages

Value Added Training Packages offer a cost-effective blend of face to face and remote training. These incorporate elements of both face to face training as well as remote.

Within each package, clients receive 2x Two Hour Face to Face Sessions as well as a single One Hour Remote Session. You can choose whether you'd prefer the remote session either at the beginning as an introductory session or saved until last as a follow-up and recap.

All of our accredited trainers are friendly, flexible and knowledgeable.

Regardless of your preference for the delivery of your sessions, all of our training programmes will be adapted to suit your style and pace of learning. Our trainers will ensure that the training you receive is practical and relevant to what you do on a daily basis, whether you work in an office, a shop or on a hospital ward. The range of software we can train on includes Dragon, Read&Write, MindView, ZoomText & many more.
